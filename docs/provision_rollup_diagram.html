ACEF Provision Rollup Algorithm

How the SDK turns individual rule results into a single compliance verdict for each regulatory provision. The algorithm checks conditions top-to-bottom and stops at the first match.

📋
What is a "provision"? A provision is a specific legal requirement — like EU AI Act Article 9 (risk management) or Article 13 (transparency). Each provision has multiple rules that check for specific evidence. After all rules run, this algorithm collapses their results into one verdict.
